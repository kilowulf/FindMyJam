Basic FAQ
What is Find My Jam?

Find My Jam is an all-in-one resource for music fans to find concerts to attend and for artists to create listings for their concerts. Our advanced algorithm finds concert listings from all over the Web and posts them on the FMJ site. This functionality combined with user-created listings means that users can find concerts ranging from top national artists to local artists. The filtering options on our search/browse page ensures that you can find the concert that is right for you!

I bought a ticket to a concert listed on Find My Jam and it was a fake ticket/ I was scammed! Can you help me get a refund?

We guarantee the authenticity of all ticket links on our official concert listings. If you bought a fake ticket through one of our official listings, please contact us and we will refund you. However, we are not responsible for user-created concert listings. By purchasing a ticket from a link on a user-created listing, you assume the responsibility for all risks related to that ticket purchase. Please see your user agreement for details.

My favorite local band’s concerts are not listed on your site! Can you post their concerts on the site?

Our algorithm, although sophisticated, may not pick up bands with limited web presence. Please reach out to your local band and ask them to create an account on Find My Jam and post their concert as a user-created listing. Anyone with a FMJ account can create a concert listing.

I am an artist who wants to create a concert listing, but I can’t find a link to create a listing. How do I create a concert listing?

A free user account is required to create a concert listing. Please create an account, then look on your account page under the tab “My Concert Listings”. There will be a link there to create a listing.
